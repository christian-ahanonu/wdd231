Overview

Finder

Finder represent a community of people that embodies exploration.

Site Purpose

The website will provide information on upcoming events, concerts, or community gatherings in the area.
It will cater to people who want to explore what events are available in their community.

Scenarios

  • 1. What are the most popular events happening this weekend in my area?
  • 2. How can I purchase tickets for the upcoming concert at the local amphitheater?
  • 3. Are there any family-friendly community gatherings scheduled for next month?
